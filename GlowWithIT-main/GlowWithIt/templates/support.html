Personal Safety

Essential Safety Strategies

Practical, proven techniques to help Melbourne’s night workers stay safe—grounded in expert advice and real night-shift experience.

Stay alert. Stay visible. Stay connected.

  • Maintain situational awareness — head up, limit phone use on quiet streets, and trust your instincts if something feels off.
  • Choose well-lit, busy routes — even if they add a few minutes. Avoid shortcuts through parks, alleys, or isolated areas.
  • Travel in pairs or with friends — Travel with colleagues when possible, establish check-in routines, and know who to call in different situations.
  • Public transport & rideshare — use trusted services, share your trip details, and stay aware of your surroundings.
Quick prep: Before your shift, share your route and expected arrival time with a trusted friend or family member.
